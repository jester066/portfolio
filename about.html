About El-Naseer Global Services Gusau and It's Innovative IT Solutions

EL-NASEER GLOBAL SERVICES GUSAU (computer services, training and general services). is a locally owned ICT center along the ever busy Unguwar Dan Baba Area Gusau, Zamfara state planned to be the best franchise in the city through creative approach of energized management and staff providing a combination of excellent internet and computer services that is involved in Website design, hosting and development, Software and application development and installation, Cybercafé outlet, repair and maintenance of electronic gadgets including; computers and their accessories. Other additional services also includes: printing, Photocopies, Lamination, sales of bulk SMS unit, POS Services, Printing of recharge Cards, DSTV and general satellite television installation services. at value pricing, satisfaction, reliability, exciting packaging, and comfortable environment for esteem customers as a solution to increasing demand for easily accessible ICT services for day-to day modern & technology related activities along the location, saving customers time, cost and energy of visiting distanced places. Our main priority is to expand out training and business center for our effort will be a further development of more retail outlets in the surrounding areas.

MISSION STATEMENT:
Our main focus will be serving high-quality works at a great value. This will help in increasing ICT training in our environment, our main priority is to create job opportunities among youths.


VISION STATEMENT:
Our main goal is to be one of the most successful ICT outlets in Gusau, North and Nigeria, starting with one retail outlet located inside a major city as a "market tester." ABGS will strive to be a premier local brand in the local ICT industry. We want our customers to have the total experience when visiting our outlet.
